What is a Skunkworks Project? Does it involve petting Skunks?

Fortunately or Unfortunately, you don't have to anything with an actual Skunk. A skunkworks project is a project developed by a small and loosely structured group of people who research and develop a project primarily for the sake of radical innovation.

AI Skunkworks at Northeastern University is a group of people who research and develop Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning projects primarily for the sake of innovation and learning. We provide open-mic, mentorship, workshops, seminars, hack-a-thons, and events that assist those exploring the edges of AI.

Pramod Pai
Master in Information Systems at Northeastern University

"I first found out about AI Skunkworks on LinkedIn. I had previously been involved in the field of machine learning and data science and joining AI Skunkworks seemed like an exciting opportunity at the time. I didn’t know what to expect from the group initially. There was a meeting prior to the start of the fall 2020 semester where the detailed structure and plan for the group was laid out. There were many sub-groups that the members could choose from, like Model Interpretability, Auto-visualization, and Auto-reinforcement learning. I picked Model Interpretability as I had trained ML models in the past and this group matched my interest closely. But I was completely unaware of the importance and significance of interpretability, especially in the context of Industry, before joining the group. As the semester went by, I learned more and more about the different techniques in interpretability, trained models, and applied these techniques to the models. Every two weeks there was a meeting that was attended by all the group members who rarely missed these meetings. All the members in the model interpretability group were under the mentorship and guidance of Abhishek and Kartik. The mentors were excellent in paving the way for research in model interpretability. They answered all the questions that the members had and also provided state-of-the-art resources to study with, one of which was a book that they themselves had written. My journey in AI Skunkworks so far has been exceptional. I am very eager to continue my association with the group and continue learning. I’m very thankful to Professor Nik Brown for starting this research group and also to my mentors Abhishek and Kartik. I have benefitted greatly from the group in terms of the knowledge I have gained. I’m also proud to say that my work at AI Skunkworks has culminated in me getting a Programmer-analyst position at Squark.ai. I hope to make complete use of this opportunity. "
